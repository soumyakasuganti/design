Context


Circle the City is a non-profit organization that provides holistic healthcare to individuals experiencing homelessness. I volunteered to help them with the design of their website.


How I Contributed


I visited the Circle the City facility and met the core team to get a better understanding of their goals. The website needed to do a better job of communicating their story and getting people involved as volunteers or donors. I redesigned the architecture with this in mind. I also designed responsive prototypes for the website.

Outcome


The solution was received well. The redesign of the site was taken up by another team of volunteers, incorporating some of the ideas I proposed.
