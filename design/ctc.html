Circle the City

Circle the City is a non-profit organization that aims to fill the gap in healthcare for the homeless population of Maricopa county.

This project aims to make it easier for volunteers and donors to navigate their website.

I first worked on this as a class project in graduate school. To understand the organization and its operations, we were given access to the staff and limited access to patients.

At the time I redesigned their print collateral and built a working website prototype. I revisited the project later, and focused on what I had learnt from interviews with the staff and patients.

The three groups I looked at were patients, volunteers, and donors. Given the information I had, I decided to focus on the volunteer experience, but also make sure that patients and donors were able to navigate the website easily.
